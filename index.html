"Innovation & Creativity in design is a way of life"

'The 5th Element' is an idea that enhances the lifestyle concept. It emphasizes on the way people choose to live and create a space that truly defines luxury, functionality & creativity 'LFC'.

'The 5th Element' is a young Design practice works at a variety of scales & locations and approaches work without any pre conceived ideas, thus creating an expression that is unique in terms of its design, style & approach.

Our work portfolio is a mix of modern, contemporary & fusion style of designing. From the innovative concepts to extensive & practical design solutions we aim to re define the way people live, work, play & Love.

From Conceptualizing the Design to its Execution; Branding & Marketing to PR, we select the best talent and consulting teams available worldwide to form special "think tanks" that brainstorm and execute unique design solutions tailored to meet our Clients' project needs, budgets, and schedules.

With 'The 5th Element' possibilities are truly endless.

LIVING SPACES:

Our tailor made living spaces are a true essence of our core value of enhancing lifestyle concepts. From the initial client meeting through occupancy, our team works closely with our clients to identify their design goals, schedule requirements and budgetary parameters. Ultimately, we develop a design that is sensitive to the individual considerations of the project and transforms our client's vision into reality.


OUTDOOR SPACES:

Outdoor spaces continue to influence our lives and have a great impact on how we live, work, play & Love. Our holistic approach to environmental design through the understanding of different yet related disciplines based on the integration of ecology, art and design creates a perfect synergy between the inside outside living concepts.


WORK & SOCIAL SPACES:

We design for people who experience our work - customers, residents, guests, employees - to generate strategic returns for clients and emotional returns for communities. We believe that these needs are best met by creating remarkable experiences for the people who regularly engage with the places we design. We bring high quality design work through a uniquely collaborative process that works for both people & clients.
